About Bharathi Vidhyalaya Education Institutions

The School was started in 1997. Ever since the management took up the responsibility of academic administration. It has been striving to impart quality education and also to improve every student’s performance. It is a co-educational English medium school recognized by the director of Hr. Sec. School Education, Govt. of Tamilnadu, India. The extreme student community from K.G. to XII under the vigilant loving, dedicated teaching staff works tirelessly to shape the intellectual development of the students.

Important of School in Society


Our Children need to imbibe an attitude of winning and leading. There is a need for an effective collaborator who would attend to a child’s scholastic and multidimensional needs. Good Schools play the role of effective collaborator,thereby talking the responsibility of building responsible and growth-oriented citizens for the nation.

Our vision

The Vision of Education is to empower the younger generation, nurturing not only a sound body and well-trained mind but also instilling admirable habits and accomplishments. Through purposeful and noble guidance, we aspire to cultivate an integrated personality, fostering individuals who contribute meaningfully to society and embrace a lifelong journey of learning and self-discovery

Our mission

The Mission of this school is to empower all its students to be problem solvers, users of technology, effective communicators, and lifelong learners in this rapidly changing global community by providing challenging experiences in a safe, caring, supportive and cooperative environment and prepare them to be successful and happy in this highly competitive and challenging future global scenario.

Our Motto

The Motto of our School is “Born to Win” All the staff and students of the school are expected to keep the school’s motto always uppermost in their minds and ensure the standard of excellence in all their activities to ensure success and happiness in their day to day life.

From the Principal's Desk

A journey to the pinnacle of excellence has begun and it already has been very promising. Yes! BHARATHI VIDHYALAYA has witnessed yet another milestone of success in its mission. In this celebrated moment of our grade XII students having passed the board exams in flying colors, I would like to delightfully share that BHARATHI VIDHYALAYA has not just been offering a matchless schooling experience to its students for the last sixteen years, but has equally grown from strength to strength in terms of expansion of campuses and its state of the art infrastructure. It is now completely geared to operate its CBSE wing in the new campus from this academic year.

Living to Benjamin Disraeli’s words “The secret of success lies in its constancy to purpose”, this year the focus of curriculum has been meticulously designed to dilate each student’s potential for critical thinking, intellectual curiosity, and aesthetic appreciation while also helping them experience the joy of discovering their canvassed personalities.

    Rules & Regulations

    Rules & Regulations

  • Students are expected to be present in the prescribed uniform which is to be clean and tidy.
  • English is a language of communication hence all students are required to speak in English inside the school campus.
  • Students are to be present in school before the first bell.
  • Students should assemble class-wise for the morning prayer.
  • Students are expected to queue up wherever they go within the school campus.
  • When a teacher enters the class it is the duty of the students to wish the teacher “Good Morning” “Vanakkam” or “Namaste”.
  • Avoid bringing a bad name to the institutions by your deeds and actions.
  • Keep the classroom and the surroundings clean.
  • In case there is any incomplete work students must stay back and complete the work.
  • During school hours students are not supposed to meet their parents or go out of the school campus.
  • A leave letter should be provided in case of absence.
  • Students suffering from infectious diseases are not supposed to come to school.
  • Students are expected to trim their nails regularly.
  • Wearing precious ornaments is prohibited and the school cannot be held responsible in case it is lost.
  • Scribbling on a Desk, Table, Wall, Blackboard, etc is strictly against rules.
  • As per our culture, students ought to show due respect to the Principal and teachers in and outside the school campus
