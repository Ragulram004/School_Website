Facilities

Experience a transformative learning environment at our school, equipped with cutting-edge facilities. From modernized classrooms and advanced laboratories to reliable transportation services, we ensure accessibility and innovation. Our well-stocked library and comfortable hostels enhance academic pursuits, while smart classrooms integrate technology seamlessly. The expansive grounds and exciting horse riding facilities add a touch of recreation and uniqueness to our educational offerings. Join us for an enriched and vibrant educational journey.

Salient Features

Our institution offers online learning and IIT-AIEEE entrance coaching programs in collaboration with premier coaching centers. We conduct unit-wise, revision, and model tests aligned with the board exam pattern. Our evaluation scheme is crafted by subject experts, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

Smart Class

We have created a new future for students with the introduction of smart classes. It has brought technology into the classroom through visualizing systems. It has empowered the teacher with well-researched, mapped to curriculum digital modules which are projected light in the classroom to elucidate and explain the concepts. As a result, the classroom comes and the perennial tug of war between the teacher’s challenge to explain and the student’s struggle to understand is solved, and inspired participation in the journey of discovering new concepts takes place enthusiastically.
